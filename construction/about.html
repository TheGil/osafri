about us

Osafric Construction & Mining

OSAFRIC MINES (Construction & Mining) is the construction and mining company you are searching for to do with bussiness with. The company is registered under the companies’ code of Ghana(1964) Act 79. The company is registered to carry on business as construction consultants and miners.

The Company was founded by Mr. Oscar Sarfo who has more than 6 years of experience in the construction and mining industry. Through his expertise, he was able to bring the operations of the business to profitability within its first year of operations.

This young entrepreneur believes in creativity and innovations and he is in the person of Oscar Sarfo. He is the main brain behind the establishment of Osafric Mines and is currently the chief executive officer (C.E.O) of the company. He holds Bachelor`s degree in Management and Computer studies, certificate for crane and folk lift operations.
